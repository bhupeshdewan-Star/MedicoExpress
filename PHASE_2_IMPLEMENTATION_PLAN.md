- Deliver Tasks 6-45 (40 outputs) by July 31
- Maintain quality standards; accept minor scope reduction

**Option 2: Resource Surge**

**Option 3: Phased Release**
- Release completed batches even if Batch 4 not ready
- Batch 1-3 release by Jul 25 (35 outputs)
- Batch 4 (15 outputs) follow on schedule in August

### If Quality Gates Fail (>5% Rework)

**Option 1: Extend QA Phase**
- Batch affected stays in QA longer
- Do not release until 100% gates passed
- Compensate by compressing next batch timeline

**Option 2: Deep Root Cause Analysis**
- Identify SOP deficiency or subagent training gap
- Implement corrective action immediately
- Retest affected outputs

**Option 3: Expert Escalation**
- CMO/Medical Expert reviews all outputs personally
- Identifies patterns; recommends process improvements
- Integrate feedback into remaining batches

### If Compliance Exception Occurs

**Immediate Response:**
1. Stop all releases
2. Full compliance review of all outputs in flight
3. CMO + Regulatory Officer + Legal consult
4. Corrective action plan

**Remediation:**
- Affected output(s) reworked
- Root cause addressed in process/training
- All outputs re-validated before resuming releases
