```

├─ Type: Randomized, double-blind, parallel-group
├─ Duration: 8 weeks treatment
├─ Patient Population: Erosive GERD patients (Los Angeles Grade C-D)

├─ Randomization: 1:1:1 (vonoprazan 20mg, omeprazole 20mg, placebo)
├─ Primary Endpoint: Healing rate at Week 8

├─ Age: 18-75 years
├─ Gender: Mixed (52% female)
├─ GERD Duration: ≥6 months
├─ Endoscopic Grade: All Los Angeles Grade C-D (moderate-severe)

├─ Mean age: 54 years

│  └─ Grade D: 35%

└─ Prior PPI use: 60% (pre-treatment documented)

HERO TRIAL RESULTS - PRIMARY ENDPOINT:

Primary Efficacy (Healing at Week 8):
├─ Vonoprazan 20mg:    94% (342/363 patients)
├─ Omeprazole 20mg:    87% (318/366 patients)

│  ├─ Absolute Difference: +7% (94% vs 87%)

│  └─ 95% CI: 2.1%-11.9%

   ├─ Absolute Difference: +53% (94% vs 41%)
   ├─ P-value: <0.0001 (highly significant)
   └─ Demonstrates superiority to both comparators

SECONDARY ENDPOINTS:

├─ Vonoprazan: Median 4 weeks

├─ Difference: -1 week faster (20% acceleration)
├─ P-value: <0.001
```
