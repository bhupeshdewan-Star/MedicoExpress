## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### The Opportunity
Vonoprazan (Takecoda) represents the first Potassium-Competitive Acid Blocker (P-CAB) approved in the USA, fundamentally changing the competitive landscape of acid suppression therapy. In a market dominated by PPIs for 25+ years, vonoprazan offers a demonstrable clinical advantage: **faster healing (3 weeks vs 5 weeks) with superior efficacy (94% vs 87%)**.

- **Total Addressable Market:** $3.8B (US GERD market)
- **Primary Opportunity:** 600K-1M PPI-refractory patients + 2-3M rapid-relief seekers
- **Market Entry:** 100% market share in P-CAB class (first-mover advantage)
- **Growth Potential:** $500M-$2B peak annual revenue (Year 3-5)

### The Competitive Advantage
```
VONOPRAZAN vs OMEPRAZOLE (Market Leader):
├─ Efficacy: 94% vs 87% healing (+7% absolute, NNT=14)
├─ Speed: 3 weeks vs 5 weeks (+40% faster)
├─ Mechanism: Direct P-CAB action vs slow PPI prodrug activation
├─ Safety: Equivalent (9-year Japan data)
├─ Cost: $150-250/month (premium to generics, discount to brands)
└─ Market Position: Disruptive innovation in mature category
```

1. **PPI-Refractory GERD** (primary, highest value)
