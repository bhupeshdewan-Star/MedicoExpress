### Reimbursement Strategy

**Insurance Negotiation Framework:**
```
Value Proposition for Payers:
├─ Faster healing (3 weeks vs 5 weeks) = fewer office visits
├─ Reduced hospitalization risk (refractory = complex patients)
├─ Improved patient adherence (speed + efficacy)
├─ Avoided escalation to surgery
└─ Cost-effectiveness: Premium drug cost < surgical intervention cost

Prior Authorization (PA) Criteria:
├─ Patient fails 8+ weeks of standard PPI therapy at therapeutic dose
├─ Patient has documented erosive GERD on endoscopy
├─ Physician is gastroenterologist or ID specialist

└─ PA approval = clear refractory GERD pathway

Formulary Positioning:
├─ Tier 3 (Specialty tier) on most plans
├─ Prior authorization required (gatekeeping for refractory segment)
├─ Patient copay: $50-150/month (manageable for specialty drug)
├─ Coverage: Expected 80-90% of commercial plans within Year 1
```
