## 7. Important Separation Rules

- Product Appraisal, Product Monograph, CME Slides, HCP Slide Decks, and Product Training Slides are separate activities.
- Similar outputs do not justify sharing one SOP when purpose, audience, source requirements, compliance boundaries, or approval routes differ.
- Shared controls should be maintained as reusable policies or standards, then referenced by each activity-specific package.
- The catalogue will expand as additional Medical Department activities are identified.
