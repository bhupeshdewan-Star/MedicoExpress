## Reason for Requalification

The first Product Monograph outputs were assessed by the owner as 2/10 because they functioned as executive evidence summaries rather than exhaustive HCP scientific monographs. The activity was therefore rebuilt rather than incrementally expanded.

The revised standard treats the Product Monograph as the product's detailed scientific knowledge resource. It must help HCPs understand the disease, molecule, evidence, safety, practical use, place in therapy, and limitations without using unsupported or manipulative persuasion.

## Evidence and Agent Model

The requalification used parallel specialist evidence reviews for:

- Trelagliptin evidence, including PubMed, PubMed Central, CDSCO, PMDA, Indian and international trials, systematic review evidence, safety, and guideline context.
- Seratrodast evidence, including CDSCO, PMDA, Japanese product information, Indian and international studies, hepatic safety, and guideline context.
- Independent medical-writing and compliance architecture review.

Google Scholar is permitted for discovery and citation chaining only. Original publications, regulator records, and stable source documents remain the evidence anchors.
