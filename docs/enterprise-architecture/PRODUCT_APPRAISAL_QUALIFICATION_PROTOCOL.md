# Product Appraisal Qualification Protocol

**Document status:** Active  
**Protocol ID:** QUAL-MA-PA-001  
**Version:** 0.1  
**Qualification date:** 2026-06-06

## Objective

Evaluate whether the Product Appraisal Activity Governance Package can generate useful, evidence-grounded, controlled Word outputs for two materially different pharmaceutical products.

## Test Runs

| Run | Product | Test challenge |
|---|---|---|
| PA-Q1 | Trelagliptin, requested as Trilagliptin | Name clarification, limited geography-specific approval, incomplete commercial and operational data |
| PA-Q2 | Empagliflozin | Broad evidence base, multiple indications, mature competitive market, complex benefit-risk profile |

## Intended Use

Internal preliminary portfolio and medical-strategy appraisal for India. Outputs are not final Go/No-Go decisions and do not replace regulatory, patent, legal, finance, CMC, supply, safety, or commercial due diligence.

## Critical Pass Criteria

- Correct molecule identity and uncertainty handling.
- No unverified Indian approval, pricing, patent, market-share, API, or supply claims.
- Material scientific and safety conclusions supported by identifiable sources.
- Weighted score and recommendation transparently linked to data availability.
- Red flags can override numeric attractiveness.
- Evidence gaps are converted into required diligence actions.
- Output includes document control, limitations, references, and review status.
- Word document renders without clipping, overlap, or broken tables.

## Qualification Rubric

| Dimension | Weight |
|---|---:|
| SOP and activity alignment | 15 |
| Skill-framework coverage | 15 |
| Evidence quality and provenance | 20 |
| Scientific and safety accuracy | 15 |
| Data-gap and uncertainty handling | 10 |
| Decision usefulness | 10 |
| Controls, red flags, and limitations | 10 |
| Word-document usability and presentation | 5 |
| **Total** | **100** |

## Decision Thresholds

- Pass: 85 or higher with zero critical findings.
- Conditional Pass: 70-84 with zero critical findings.
- Fail: below 70 or any unresolved critical finding.
