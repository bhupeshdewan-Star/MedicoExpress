## 1. PURPOSE

To create regular (monthly/quarterly) medical newsletters for HCP communication, covering product updates, scientific literature summaries, clinical practice guidance, and regulatory updates, while maintaining compliance with promotional guidelines.

---

## 2. SCOPE

Applies to:
- Monthly/quarterly HCP newsletters
- Disease-specific medical updates
- Product safety updates
- Clinical practice guidance
- Literature highlights
- Regulatory milestone announcements

---

## 3. QUALITY GATES

1. **Content Relevance** → Topics current and relevant to target HCP audience
2. **Evidence-Based** → All clinical claims substantiated by published literature
3. **On-Label** → Product information consistent with approved indication
4. **Balanced** → Benefits and risks discussed proportionally
5. **Regulatory Compliance** → UCPMP/ABPI/FDA-compliant
6. **Professionalism** → Tone appropriate for HCP audience; free of hype

---

## 4. NEWSLETTER STRUCTURE (4-6 articles)

### Article 1: Featured Clinical Topic (800-1000 words)
- Deep dive into therapeutic area topic
- Current clinical evidence
- Practice guidance
- References for further reading

### Article 2: Product Update or New Data (500-700 words)
- Recent regulatory approval or milestone
- New clinical data presentation at congress
- Safety update
- Prescribing guidance update

### Article 3: Literature Highlights (400-600 words)
- Summary of 2-3 recent published studies relevant to product/indication
- Clinical implications
- Links to full publications

### Article 4: Case Study or Clinical Perspective (600-800 words)
- Real-world clinical case (anonymized)
- Expert commentary
- Key learning points

### Article 5: Regulatory or Clinical Guidance (400-500 words)
- FDA/EMA guidance updates
- New clinical trial designs
- Registry or real-world evidence initiatives

### Section: Company Updates (200-300 words)
- Product availability updates
- Patient assistance program information
- Medical information contact details
- Upcoming scientific meetings/events

---

## 5. PROCEDURE: TWO-PASS DELIVERY

### PASS 1: DRAFT NEWSLETTER (Builder Agent)
- Research topic and gather literature
- Draft 4-6 articles with citations
- Include images, tables where appropriate
- Format for email/web distribution

### PASS 2: REVIEW
- **QA Agent:** Verify completeness, clarity, structure
- **Compliance Agent:** Verify substantiation, on-label, fair-balance
- **Medical Editor:** Review scientific accuracy and tone
- **Revision:** Up to 2 cycles before escalation

---

## 6. APPROVAL & RELEASE

- **Editorial Lead Sign-off:** Content quality and relevance
- **Medical Officer Sign-off:** Scientific accuracy
- **Regulatory Officer Sign-off:** UCPMP/ABPI/FDA compliance
