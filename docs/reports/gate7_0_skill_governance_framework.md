# World-Class Skill Governance Framework — Gate 7.0 Foundation Directive

## 1. Governance Principles
This framework establishes the governance guidelines under which all dynamic skills within ClinCommand OS™ are designed, loaded, audited, and certified.

## 2. Eight-Layer Skill Intelligence Standard
To be activated on the platform, every repository-driven skill must strictly satisfy:
- **Layer 1: Strategic Purpose**: Declares why the skill exists, who uses it, business goals, and success metrics.
- **Layer 2: Expert Knowledge Model**: Encapsulates clinical, regulatory, or business expertise. Generic summaries or chatbots are rejected.
- **Layer 3: Operating Principles**: Maps mandatory constraints, prohibited actions, assumptions, and escalation limits.
- **Layer 4: Output Architecture**: Directs output structures, including required sections, evidence tables, and analysis matrices.
- **Layer 5: Explainability**: Attaches a transparent metadata manifest containing purpose, inputs, limitations, and traceability details.
- **Layer 6: Compliance**: Binds outputs to regulatory standards (FDA, EMA, GxP, 21 CFR Part 11).
- **Layer 7: Quality Gates**: Asserts completeness, evidence validation, consistency checks, and traceability checks.
- **Layer 8: Reusability**: Ensures skills operate as enterprise assets, independent of one specific product, project, or user.
