# GxP Report — ClinCommand OS™ Gate 6.2 Customer Success Framework

## Overview
This report establishes the customer success framework, onboarding programs, user training models, adoption metrics tracking, and administrator enablement guidelines for ClinCommand OS™ SaaS customers.

---

## 1. Onboarding Program
* **Phase 1: Setup:** Tenant initialization, domain white-labeling, and core identity integrations.
* **Phase 2: Configuration:** Active skills selection, SOP alignment matrices mapping, and RAG knowledge collections loading.
* **Phase 3: Launch:** Go-live sign-offs and support activation.

---

## 2. Training Program
* **GxP Module:** Mandatory compliance courses for clinical users.
* **Electronic Signature Module:** 21 CFR Part 11 and EU Annex 11 signature rule training.
* **Audit Trail Usage:** Administrator training on verify tools and output reconstruction.

---

## 3. Adoption Program & Governance Awareness
* **Usage Telemetry:** Active dashboards track Daily Active Users (DAU), average sessions, and execute volume.
* **Governance Checkpoints:** Real-time feedback guides users through domain isolation gates and electronic signature requirements.

---

## 4. Customer Success KPIs
* **Metric 1:** Platform Adoption Rate (target > 85.0%).
* **Metric 2:** Daily Active Users (DAU) count against seats licensed.
* **Metric 3:** Support Incident volume and SLA response times.
* **Metric 4:** Training completion rates (target 100% for all clinical operators).
