# GxP Report — ClinCommand OS™ Gate 6.2 Revenue Operations Framework

## Overview
This report details the subscription lifecycle management, contract frameworks, renewal processes, customer expansion controls, and de-provisioning offboarding governance for ClinCommand OS™ SaaS models.

---

## 1. Subscription Lifecycle
* **Tier Management:** Subscription levels are structured as Silver, Gold, or Platinum, with verified boundaries on user seat count.
* **Licensing Enforcement:** Automated seat enforcement checks prevent tenants from exceeding contract boundaries.
* **Pricing Matrices:** Standard subscription rates are verified and linked to billing automation systems.

---

## 2. Contract Lifecycle
* **Contract Integration:** Customer agreements specify start dates, end dates, and renewal clauses.
* **Approval Gates:** Contracts require legal and commercial sign-off before tenant state transitions to ACTIVE.

---

## 3. Renewal Process
* **Renewal Timelines:** Renewal cycles prompt review cycles 90 days before contract expiration.
* **Auto-Renew Policies:** Active contracts transition to renewal states unless cancellation notice is registered.

---

## 4. Customer Expansion Process
* **Seat Expansion:** Tenants can request additional user seats dynamically, subject to pricing adjustments and security checks.
* **Domain Upgrades:** Expansion to additional clinical domains (e.g. adding Pharmacovigilance to Medical Affairs) is managed through secure catalog additions.

---

## 5. Offboarding Controls
* **Tenant Deactivation:** Upon contract termination, tenant status transitions to SUSPENDED.
* **Data Retention Policies:** Tenant data remains locked for 30 days before archival.
* **Secure Archival:** Archives are encrypted and stored according to regulatory record retention guidelines.
